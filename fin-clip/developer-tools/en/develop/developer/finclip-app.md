## [\#](https://super-apps.ai/mop/document/en/develop/developer/finclip-app.html\#_1-introduction) 1\. Introduction

You can use FinClip App (also referred to as "FinClip App") to learn about FinClip's example Mini-Programs and feature introductions via this mobile application, and you can also preview and manage your Mini-Programs.

![](https://super-apps.ai/mop/document/assets/img/app01.69672d01.jpg)
FinClip App primarily includes the following functions:

- View actual demonstrations of Mini-Programs in business scenarios via "Popular Mini-Programs";
- View actual demonstrations of official components and the practical usage of interface-type components via "Example Demonstrations";
- View introductions and demonstrations of Mini-Program-specific features via "Interactive Pages";
- View the status of all Mini-Programs under your account via "Manage Mini-Programs".

![](https://super-apps.ai/mop/document/assets/img/app02.bcf3ff3f.png)

As shown in the figure above, these are the "FinClip Mini-Program Component Examples" and "Mini-Program Version Management View" respectively. Here you can learn in more detail about the Mini-Program features supported by FinClip, and check the version status of your own Mini-Programs.

> To view the FinClip App user guide and login methods, please [click here](https://super-apps.ai/mop/document/introduce/functionDescription/finclip.html)
